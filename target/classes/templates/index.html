We rent canoes to inexperienced parties as well as to the experienced canoeist. You can leave you car at the take-out point and we will shuttle you up river to the put-in point.
For down river trips, we will pick you up at the end of your trip and shuttle you back to your vehicle. We provide approved life jackets and paddles which are included in the cost of the canoe rental.
